We breed several well thought out litters each year, and have waiting lists for both show and companion puppies.  Our puppies are home raised and vet checked before leaving. 

Our companion puppies are ready to go around eight to ten weeks of age.  Our show puppies usually stay a while longer.  We carefully screen prospective buyers, as we are looking to place our puppies in the best possible homes, with the understanding that the puppy is a lifetime commitment. 

We guarantee our puppies to be in good health at the time of sale and provide a two-year temperament and genetic health guarantee.  We are members in good standing of the following clubs:  

Cenntennial Shetland Sheepdog Club American Shetland Sheepdog Association We are also Breeders of Merit with American Kennel Club.
