We are a small breed kennel located in Craig, CO

We have one to two litters a year. All puppies are evaluated for structure and temperament at about 8 weeks old.

We breed our litters to produce show quality dogs. Shelites are size restricated and change Shelites are size restricated and change during adolescence. As such, we may keep them for four to six months prior to placing them as pets.

All our puppies will be well socialized the same as our show prospects. Our goal is to produce sound structure, great temperament and healthy champions.

Occasionally we place show prospects to show homes and performance enthusiast, as well as beautiful bright companions.

All our pet puppies are sold on a spay/neuter contract. Check our Nursery for current and planned litters and a puppy application. We join many other Sheltie breeders in the importance of health and testing of our dogs. Health and temperament rank among our highest concerns when breeding Shelties. The shy Selties is now a characteristic of the paste, because we choose the Sire and Dam carefully. All Aspenleaf adult Shelties are tested for hips OFA, vWD, Thyroid and Eye certification.

The Shetland Sheepdog is intensely loyal, affectionate, and responsive to his owner.

Right Breed for You?

Shelties love their families, but may be reserved at first with strangers. As a herding dog, they love to run and play with a built in herding instinct. Being a very vocal breed they may bark if untrained. Shelties thrive on the farm, but adapt to many living situations if given proper exercise and attention. The breed's dense double coat requires regular maintenance.(Weekly to Bi-weekly brushing)

We breed several well thought out litters each year, and have waiting lists for both show and companion puppies.  Our puppies are home raised and vet checked before leaving. 

Our companion puppies are ready to go around eight to ten weeks of age.  Our show puppies usually stay a while longer.  We carefully screen prospective buyers, as we are looking to place our puppies in the best possible homes, with the understanding that the puppy is a lifetime commitment.  We guarantee our puppies to be in good health at the time of sale and provide a two-year temperament and genetic health guarantee.  We are members in good standing of the following clubs:
